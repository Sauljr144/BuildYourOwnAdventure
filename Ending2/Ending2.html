Guilty Emily

Emily admits to killing her father. She found out she wasn't getting anything from the family inheritance; she would not even be getting their mother's jewelry! She decided to stop him before he could rewrite it, only to find out the amendment had already done and was then stored in the safe. She took the will out and destroyed it, taking the necklace along with it in order to make it look like a run of the mill theft. The police are called and Emily is escorted to jail.
